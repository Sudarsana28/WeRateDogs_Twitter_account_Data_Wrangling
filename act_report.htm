Data Analysis on the WeRateDogs Twitter account:

WeRateDogs is a Twitter account that rates people's dogs with a humorous comment about their dog.  It was started in 2015 by college student named Matt Nelson and has received international media coverage both for its popularity and for the attention drawn to social media. It has acquired more than 7 million followers since its debut. 

This account also had a unique rating system. The ratings almost always have a denominator of 10. But the numerators are almost always greater than 10 with ratings such as 11/10, 12/10, 13/10, etc. Why? Because "they're good dogs Brent." Now, this statement is a sarcastic answer given from the WeRateDogsTwitter account during a celebrated exchange in which the account shut down a person taking issue with their rating system. This exchange (conversation) was also a massive success for the account with the answer gaining popularity as ‘One of the best memes of 2016’ and the account gained 50,000 followers as a result of the exchange.

I happened to do a data analysis project on this account’s data and discovered various interesting insights. I have also included some pictorial representations to make the entire data visualization process both colourful and meaningful.

WeRateDogs also have their dogs classified under 4 dog stages namely ‘doggo’,’puppo’,’floofer’ and ‘pupper’. You may have to refer to the  WeRateDogs own dogtionary in their book sold by Amazon, to understand what each dog stage stands for https://www.amazon.com/WeRateDogs-Most-Hilarious-Adorable-Youve/dp/1510717145

The ‘doggo' dog stage has the highest number of favourite counts with the number of favourite counts being 160816. It also has the highest number of retweet counts. The number of retweet counts being 80497. As seen below, Twitter from iPhone is the source for maximum number of tweets. The tweet counts from other sources were very insignificant compared to the Twitter for iPhone reaching a count of 1434.

 

 

Among the different ratings given to Dogs, the rating of 12/10 looks like the most popular rating of WeRateDogs. Here, I have made the calculations for ratings as 12/10= 1.2, displaying their respective quotients as the ratings.

1.2 tops the list of ratings with over 350 dogs rated so followed by the ratings of 1.0 and 1.1.

 

 

An image prediction algorithm was used to predict the dog images from the account. It was interesting to find that Golden Retriever was the most predicted dog breed with 138 dogs coming under this prediction followed by Labrador Retriever, Pembroke and the Chihuahuas. This shows how much people love their Golden Retrievers.
